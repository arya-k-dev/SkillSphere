FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does skill exchange work?

You list what you can teach and what you want to learn. SkillSphere helps you find a partner with reciprocal goals.

Is SkillSphere free?

Yes. SkillSphere is designed around mutual skill exchange, not paid programs.

How does matching work?

Matching compares teachable skills, learning goals, categories, and profile signals to suggest suitable partners.

Can I reject a request?

Yes. You stay in control of the requests you accept, reject, or revisit later.

How do ratings work?

Ratings and reviews help the community understand exchange quality and build trust over time.

Can I learn more than one skill?

Yes. You can explore multiple skills and connect with different learning partners as your goals expand.
